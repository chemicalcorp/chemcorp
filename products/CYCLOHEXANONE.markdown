description: Cyclohexanone is used in organic synthesis, particularly in the production of adipic acid and caprolactam (ca. 95%), polyvinyl chloride and its copolymers and methacrylate ester polymers. The great majority of cyclohexanone is consumed in the production of precursors to Nylon 6,6 (adipic acid) and Nylon 6 (oxime). Additional uses include wood stains, paint and varnish removers, degreasing of metals, polishes, levelling agents, dyeing and delustering silk, lubricating oil additives, cellulosics, natural and synthetic resins, waxes, fats, etc.
