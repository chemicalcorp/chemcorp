description: Its primary use is as a solvent in the synthesis of Active Pharmaceutical ingredients (API) and crop protection ingredients. Its other uses are in the electronic industry, in formulation of mixtures, as gas stabiliser in acetylene cylinders, in the production of medical devices, as cleaning solvent, as laboratory chemical etc.
